About Me
Thanks for your interest, here is a quick story of me and this website.
Asmin Giri, also known as Emikid, is a Nepalese rapper, lyricist, author, and music producer from Lamahi, Dang. He was born on 1st June 2007 in Lamahi. He was from a society where people treat rappers as bad influencers/people. He was inspired by his uncle's books to write his own book. He was also inspired by a book named "The Rudest Book Ever" by Shwetabh Gangwar to write his own book. That is the reason, his first book's name little bit match with that book.
Story of my stage name
Emikid, this name tell the half story itself. He was inspired by Eminem so, he copy/use Emi from Eminem. Juice Wrld was he one of the first rapper whome he used to listen. The first rapper whose biography he read was Juice Wrld. He read that Juice Wrld initial name was Juice The Kidd so, he cope/use Kidd as (kid) from Juice Wrld. That’s way he chose Emikid as his stage name to give respect to his first inspirations.
